Bloat

Bloat is a condition that is much more common in deep-chested large breed dogs - which includes German Shepherds. 

Bloat can turn deadly very quickly for your dog. Know what causes it, and what the symptoms are, and how you can help prevent it.

History of the German Shepherd 

 In 1889 a Captain Max von Stephanitz began the standardization of the breed. His inspiration came from a dog show in which a medium-sized yellow and gray wolf-like dog caught his attention. This dog was supple and powerful, possessed endurance, steadiness, and intelligence. Working as a sheepherder, it required no training other than direction.                

The Captain purchased this dog, who became the first registered German Shepherd Dog.
